Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that plans change. Our policy is designed to be fair to both guests and our hotel partners.

1. Cancellation Tiers

  • Free Cancellation: Full refund if cancelled at least 48 hours before the check-in time.
  • 50% Refund: If cancelled between 24 and 48 hours before check-in.
  • Non-Refundable: No refund if cancelled less than 24 hours before check-in or in case of a "No Show."

2. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed immediately by our system but may take 5-7 business days to reflect in your original payment method depending on your bank.
