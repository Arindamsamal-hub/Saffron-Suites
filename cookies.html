Cookie Policy

StayFinder uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and personalize content.

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and login status.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function (e.g., secure login, booking flow). These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site (e.g., page views, time spent) so we can improve performance.

3. Managing Cookies

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of the booking system.
